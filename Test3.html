Here are the ingredients for basic risotto

1 1/2 cups of Arborio rice

4 cups of chicken or vegetable broth

1/2 cup of dry white wine

1/2 cup of grated Parmesan cheese

2 tablespoons of unsalted butter

1 small onion, chopped

2 cloves of garlic, minced

1/2 teaspoon of salt

1/4 teaspoon of black pepper

Optional ingredients

1/2 cup of chopped mushrooms

1/2 cup of frozen peas

1/2 cup of diced tomatoes

1/4 cup of chopped fresh herbs (such as parsley, basil, or thyme)

1. In a medium-sized saucepan, heat the chicken or vegetable broth over medium heat until it simmers. Keep the broth warm on low heat.

2. In a large heavy-bottomed pot or Dutch oven, heat the olive oil and butter over medium heat until the butter is melted. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic and saut√© until the onion is translucent and fragrant, about 3-4 minutes.

3. Add the Arborio rice to the pot and stir to coat with the butter and oil mixture. Continue stirring until the rice becomes slightly translucent around the edges, about 2-3 minutes.

4. Pour in the white wine and stir until it is absorbed by the rice. Then, add a ladleful of warm broth to the pot and stir until it is absorbed by the rice. Repeat this process, adding one ladleful of broth at a time and stirring constantly until the rice is cooked through and the mixture is creamy, about 20-25 minutes.

5. Stir in the grated Parmesan cheese and season with salt and black pepper to taste.

6. Optional: If you want to add any optional ingredients such as chopped mushrooms or peas, add them at this stage and stir until heated through.

7. Remove the pot from heat and let the risotto rest for a few minutes before serving. Serve hot with additional grated Parmesan cheese and chopped fresh herbs (optional) on top.
